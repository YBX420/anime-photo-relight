🌬️ Fan Feng Shui

It's a sweltering British summer and you've got no air-con. Only fans. Place them, aim them, and find the layout that keeps you coolest — by day and by night.

  • 🖱️ Tap the floor to drop a fan, then drag to aim it.
  • 🪟 At night open the window and blow cool outside air in.
  • ☀️ By day the sun heats the room — close up and let the breeze chill your skin instead.

Outdoor temperature is pulled live from your chosen city. Airflow is a real incompressible fluid simulation.
